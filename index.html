ABOUT

ARMISTICE (Ross) is an up & coming Producer/DJ based in London.

Only 20, he has stormed his way through the scene playing at some of the biggest and best Venues in the world inc. XOYO, Ministry of Sound, Fire & Lightbox, Space, Ibiza/Mallorca Rocks, Canal Mills, Project Mallorca, Twickenham Stadium, The O2 Arena, MiNT Warehouse & many more.

Born and Raised in Yorkshire, Ross started his DJ career in Leeds. It wasn’t long before his Energetic house sets lead him to the Capital where his name has grown into an established brand bringing his own event ARMISTICE & FRIENDS to Brixton hitting capacity on the launch event.

Also a keen producer Ross is soon to be releasing his own highly anticipated original music taking influences from his years of playing alongside some of the best DJ’s around the world.
